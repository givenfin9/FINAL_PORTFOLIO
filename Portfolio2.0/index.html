About Me

I'm Frolov Oleksandr, a multidisciplinary creative technologist based in Kiev, Ukraine. With over 8 years of experience at the intersection of design and technology, I specialize in crafting immersive digital experiences that merge art, code, and interactivity.

My journey began with a degree in Digital Media Design from the University of Kiev, followed by a Master's in Interactive Technologies from the Royal College of Art in London. This unique combination of education allows me to bridge the gap between technical implementation and creative vision.

I've had the privilege of working with brands like Nike, Google Arts & Culture, and the Serpentine Galleries, creating installations and digital experiences that push the boundaries of what's possible with web technologies.

Projects

MetaSpace

A virtual gallery space combining Three.js, WebGL and multiplayer support, allowing artists to showcase their work in customizable 3D environments with real-time visitor interaction.

Echoes

An interactive sound/light installation powered by Max/MSP and Arduino, exhibited at Lisbon's Museum of Art, reacting to visitor movements with generative audio-visual patterns.

Onda

A fluid motion surf brand site with parallax scroll, dynamic color themes, and animations that respond to scroll velocity, built with GSAP and WebGL shaders.

Lumina

A poetic user-driven story rendered with motion graphics and spatial sound design, exploring narrative through generative systems and machine learning text analysis.

Neon Dreams

An AR filter series for Instagram that transforms urban environments into cyberpunk landscapes using real-time image processing and geolocation data.

Chroma

A web-based tool for designers to create and share color palettes with AI suggestions, collaborative features, and accessibility checking.
